Your participation in this survey is entirely voluntary. We will not share your responses with any third party. Any findings will be reported in aggregate and anonymized form only.
About this Study
Today, patients are largely left on their own to navigate supportive care — facing predatory marketing, products that aren't curated or appropriate for their specific condition, and no clear path to coverage through insurance or supplemental benefits. At the same time, providers have little visibility into whether patients are actually accessing the non-pharmaceutical products they recommend, receiving the right items, or getting any financial relief to help them do so.

Wells Market Co. is contemplating building a clinically validated supportive care marketplace designed to close that gap: giving providers a simple way to recommend the products best suited for each patient's care, helping to ensure access to those items either through delivery at point of care or at home, and automatically applying any insurance or supplemental benefit coverage — so that the right product reaches the right patient, at the right time, with as little friction and out-of-pocket cost as possible.

Your candid perspective will shape what and how we build this solution.

Why this matters
Many supportive care products are FSA/HSA eligible under post-CARES Act rules. Medicare Advantage and Medicaid managed care plans are building supplemental benefit catalogs — and employer benefit programs are seeking meaningful offerings for employees managing medically complex / chronic conditions. Your perspective on coverage shapes how we design the payment architecture.
